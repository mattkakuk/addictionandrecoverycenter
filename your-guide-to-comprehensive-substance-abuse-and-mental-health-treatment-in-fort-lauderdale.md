## **Understanding Substance Abuse Treatment in Fort Lauderdale**

### **What Does Substance Abuse Treatment Entail?**

Substance abuse treatment involves a structured approach to help individuals
overcome dependency on drugs or alcohol. This often combines medical
assistance with counseling, ensuring both physical detox and psychological
support.

## **Medically Assisted Treatment (MAT) in Fort Lauderdale**

### **What Is MAT and How Does It Help?**

Medically Assisted Treatment combines medications with counseling to address
dependence. It’s particularly effective for opioid addiction, including
Suboxone Maintenance.

### **Our Medication Management Services**

Our physicians carefully prescribe medications like Suboxone and naltrexone to
ease withdrawal symptoms and reduce cravings, minimizing relapse risk.

### **Why Choose MAT in Fort Lauderdale?**

With trusted clinics nearby, including our facility along the Florida coast,
MAT becomes accessible and personalized.
