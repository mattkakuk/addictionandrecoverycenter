### **Behavioral Therapies: CBT, Trauma, and EMDR**

Behavioral therapies are integral to our treatment programs:

  * **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)** : This evidence-based approach helps patients recognize and change negative thought patterns that contribute to substance use.
  * **Trauma Therapy** : Many individuals struggling with addiction have underlying trauma. Addressing these issues is crucial for sustainable recovery.
  * **EMDR Therapy** : Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is a proven method for processing traumatic memories, reducing their emotional impact, and promoting healing. Our **[EMDR Therapy Fort Lauderdale](https://www.google.com/maps?cid=5174639524515557938)** sessions aim to help clients recover from past trauma and build resilience.
