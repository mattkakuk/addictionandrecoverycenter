## **Types of Substance Abuse Treatments Available**

Different treatment modalities serve different needs. Here are some effective
options available in Fort Lauderdale:

### **Detoxification and Medical Stabilization**

Detox provides a safe environment for physical withdrawal under medical
supervision. It is often the first step in recovery, especially for those with
severe substance dependencies.

### **Inpatient and Outpatient Rehabilitation**

  * **Inpatient Rehab** offers intensive care within a residential setting, ideal for those requiring comprehensive support.
  * **Outpatient Rehab** provides flexibility, allowing individuals to receive treatment while maintaining daily responsibilities.

### **Medically Assisted Treatment (MAT)**

MAT plays a vital role, especially with opioid dependency. It combines
behavioral therapy with medications to reduce cravings and withdrawal
symptoms.
