## **Medically Assisted Treatment (MAT): A Critical Component**

### **What Is Medically Assisted Treatment?**

Medically Assisted Treatment (MAT) combines the use of FDA-approved
medications with counseling and behavioral therapies to treat substance use
disorders. It’s a proven method that reduces withdrawal symptoms, decreases
cravings, and improves functional outcomes.

### **MAT in Fort Lauderdale**

In Fort Lauderdale, MAT can be a cornerstone of recovery, especially for

## **Therapeutic Modalities Supporting Mental Health**

### **CBT Therapy in Recovery**

Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a cornerstone of mental health
treatment. It helps individuals identify and challenge maladaptive thought
patterns, replacing them with healthier behaviors. CBT is effective in
addressing underlying issues contributing to substance use.

### **Trauma Therapy and EMDR in Fort Lauderdale**

Trauma often plays a significant role in substance abuse. Our trauma therapy
services, including Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR)
therapy, can help process traumatic memories believably and compassionately.
