## Types of Substance Abuse Treatment Available in Fort Lauderdale

**Substance abuse treatment** encompasses a spectrum of therapies tailored to
each individual 's needs. From detoxification to long-term inpatient or
outpatient programs, our approach is designed for safety and efficacy.

### 1\. Medical Detoxification

Detox, the first step in many treatment plans, involves supervised removal of
substances from the body. It minimizes withdrawal symptoms and ensures safety.

### 2\. Inpatient and Outpatient Rehabilitation

  * **Inpatient rehab** offers intensive, 24/7 support within a secure environment. It’s ideal for severe cases or those with co-occurring disorders.
  * **Outpatient rehab** provides flexibility for individuals with milder addiction issues who wish to maintain daily responsibilities while seeking treatment.

### 3\. Medically Assisted Treatment (MAT)

**Medically assisted treatment** combines medications with counseling to
enhance recovery outcomes. Our clinic in Fort Lauderdale offers **MAT**
options such as **Suboxone maintenance** , which helps reduce cravings and
withdrawal symptoms.

## The Role of Evidence-Based Therapies in Recovery

Effective recovery hinges on proven therapeutic modalities that address the
psychological roots of addiction. Here are some of the most impactful
therapies we incorporate:

### 1\. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT is a cornerstone in substance abuse treatment, helping individuals
recognize and change thought patterns that lead to substance use. It’s about
rewiring the brain’s responses to stress and cravings, empowering clients to
make healthier choices.

### 2\. Trauma Therapy and EMDR

Many individuals with substance use disorders have histories of trauma.
**Trauma therapy** , including **EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and
Reprocessing)** , specifically targets traumatic memories, reducing their
power and alleviating associated behaviors.

> For those seeking **EMDR therapy in Fort Lauderdale** , our center provides
expert care to facilitate healing from trauma.

### 3\. Medication Management

Medication management ensures individuals receive appropriate pharmacotherapy
to stabilize mood, reduce cravings, and prevent relapse.
