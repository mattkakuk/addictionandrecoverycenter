## **Understanding Substance Abuse and Mental Health Disorders**

### **What Are Substance Abuse Disorders?**

Substance abuse disorders encompass a range of conditions characterized by the
harmful use of substances such as alcohol, prescription medications, or
illicit drugs. These disorders not only affect physical health but also impair
emotional well-being and social functioning.

### **The Interconnection with Mental Health**

More often than not, substance abuse co-occurs with mental health issues like
depression, anxiety, or trauma. This dual diagnosis complicates treatment but
also highlights the need for integrated care that addresses both issues
simultaneously.
