## **Medically Assisted Treatment (MAT): The Gold Standard**

Medically assisted treatment (MAT) combines medication with counseling and
behavioral therapies. It’s proven to be effective, especially in managing
opioid dependency, alcohol addiction, and other substance use disorders.

### **How Does MAT Work?**

MAT stabilizes brain chemistry, reduces withdrawal symptoms, and diminishes
cravings—making recovery more manageable.

### **MAT Options in Fort Lauderdale**

  * Suboxone Maintenance
  * Methadone
  * Vivitrol
