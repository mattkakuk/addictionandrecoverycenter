## **Understanding Substance Abuse and Its Impact**

### **What Is Substance Abuse?**

Substance abuse involves the harmful or hazardous use of substances such as
alcohol, prescription medications, or illicit drugs. It affects millions of
individuals worldwide and can lead to addiction, physical health issues, and
mental health disorders.

### **The Complex Interplay of Substance Abuse and Mental Health**

Many individuals experiencing substance abuse also grapple with mental health
problems like depression, anxiety, or trauma-related disorders. This co-
occurrence requires an integrated treatment approach, emphasizing both aspects
simultaneously for effective recovery.

* * *

## **The Importance of a Holistic Approach to Treatment**

### **Why Integrated Care Matters**

Treating substance abuse in isolation rarely leads to sustained recovery. A
holistic approach considers the physical, emotional, and psychological
dimensions of addiction. It involves medication management, therapy, and
lifestyle support to foster lasting change.
