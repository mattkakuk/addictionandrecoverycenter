### 2\. How does Medically Assisted Treatment work?

MAT combines FDA-approved medications with counseling to address substance use
disorders. Medications like Suboxone help manage withdrawal symptoms and

### 3\. What is EMDR therapy, and how can it help with trauma?

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) therapy is a
psychotherapy technique designed to help individuals process traumatic
memories safely. It has proven effective in treating trauma-related mental
health conditions, which often coexist with substance use disorders.

Yes, many outpatient programs, including those at our Fort Lauderdale center,
